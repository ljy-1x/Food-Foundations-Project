I founded the Food Food Foundations project because after reading multiple national adolescent surveys I saw that poor eating habits in adolescence carried far into adulthood very often — I wanted to take action. I am in charge of managing partnerships with organizations, curriculum formatting, and teaching materials like slideshows, workshops, and activities.
