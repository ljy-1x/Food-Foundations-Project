Hi, I’m Ahnaf, co-founder of The Food Foundations Project. I oversee recruitment, scheduling, social media, website design, and volunteer coordination while also teaching sessions and helping train new volunteers. I co-founded this initiative to address the spread of nutrition misinformation and help younger students develop healthier relationships with food through clear, safe, and accurate lessons. My goal is to make nutrition education more accessible to students early enough for it to shape their lifelong habits.
